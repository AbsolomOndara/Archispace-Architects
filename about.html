SINCE 1998

Redefining East Africa's Architectural Landscape

Founded in Nairobi by Jameson David, Archisphere Architects has grown from a small design studio to one of Kenya's most respected full-service architecture firms. For over two decades, we've combined global design excellence with deep local knowledge to create spaces that inspire and endure.

Our portfolio spans commercial towers, luxury residences, hospitality venues, and public institutions across Kenya, Tanzania, Rwanda and Uganda. Each project reflects our commitment to sustainable design, cultural relevance, and technical innovation.

Our Mission

Creating Spaces That Elevate Human Experience

We believe architecture should serve people first. Our mission is to design environments that enhance quality of life, foster community, and respect our planet. Through thoughtful planning and innovative materials, we create buildings that are as functional as they are beautiful.

Sustainable Design Principles

  • Energy-efficient passive design strategies
  • Locally-sourced, eco-friendly materials
  • Water conservation systems
  • Biophilic design integration
Our Vision

Shaping Kenya's Architectural Future

We envision a future where African cities showcase world-class design that celebrates local heritage while embracing technological advancement. As industry leaders, we're committed to mentoring young architects and advocating for design excellence across the region.

OUR APPROACH

The Archisphere Design Philosophy

Client-Centered

We begin every project by deeply understanding your needs, aspirations and context. Our collaborative process ensures your vision guides every design decision.

Ecologically Smart

Our designs minimize environmental impact through passive cooling, rainwater harvesting, solar integration, and sustainable material selection.

Technologically Advanced

We leverage BIM modeling, VR walkthroughs, and parametric design tools to optimize functionality and visualize concepts before construction begins.
